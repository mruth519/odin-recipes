Southern Fried Shrimp

Servings : 4 - 6

Prep time : 20 minutes

Cook time : 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 dozen large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 teaspoon plus 1 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon plus 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • Oil for frying
    • Peanut oil (Recommended)
    • Vegetable Oil
    • Canola Oil
  • 1 cup well-shaken buttermilk
  • (Optional) 2 teaspoons Creole mustard

Instructions

  1. Place the shrimp in a medium bowl and season with 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper, and 1/2 tablespoon paprika
    2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour with the remaining salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, and paprika
  2. Heat 3 - 4 inches of oil in a large pot until very hot, about 350 degrees
  3. Pour the buttermilk (and mustard if using) over the seasoned shrimp and combine. Working in batches, use a slotted spoon to remove the shrimp from the buttermilk and transfer to the seasoned flour. Using a separate dry spoon or your fingers, toss to evenly coat.
  4. Carefully slip the battered shrimp into the fryer and fry until golden brown and crisp, about 5 minutes
  5. Use slotted spoon to scoop the finished shrimp out of pot, and drain on paper towels
